Farmers in Australia and around the world face a deeply rooted supply chain crisis.

Commercial operators are shutting down as farming becomes unprofitable
due to rising input costs, losing money just to stay in business.

The truth is we do not need to buy as much fertilizer, crop protection and seed
as we think we do. Sure, we need to optimize for yield, but is that little increase
in yield actually worth the cost?

Industrial products will only ever go up in price, with agrochemical companies
profiting millions and the farmer becoming poorer from last season.

Analyze and understand ways you can do cost reduction for your farming operation
when it comes to input costs.

We need margin optimization, not yield optimization.
